Final Project

Concept

This project explores the intersection of sound, robotics, and ritual through the creation of a mechanical sound installation based on a singing bowl. Inspired by the aesthetics of a phonograph, the device uses two motorized actuators: one to gently rub the rim of the bowl, and another to strike it at controlled intervals. By precisely modulating speed, pressure, and rhythm, the machine can produce a range of resonant tones and percussive gestures, mimicking human interaction with the instrument. The goal is to create a meditative, semi-autonomous sound sculpture that bridges traditional acoustic resonance with modern mechatronic control.
